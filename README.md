# 🍕 Pizza Sales Analytics Dashboard | SQL, Python & Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

Developed an end-to-end Pizza Sales Analytics solution using SQL, Python, and Power BI to analyze sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, product popularity, and revenue trends from transactional sales data.

The project combines data analysis, business intelligence, and interactive visualization to transform raw sales records into actionable insights. It enables stakeholders to monitor KPIs, identify top-performing products, understand customer preferences, and make data-driven decisions that improve profitability and operational efficiency.

### Key Highlights

* Analyzed 21K+ customer orders and 50K+ pizzas sold.
* Generated insights from $817K+ in total revenue.
* Performed business analysis using SQL queries.
* Conducted Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) using Python and Pandas.
* Built interactive dashboards using Power BI, DAX, and Power Query.
* Identified best-selling and underperforming products.
* Analyzed sales trends across categories, sizes, months, and weekdays.
* Developed KPI-driven reports to support business decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

Pizza businesses generate thousands of sales transactions daily. Without proper analytics, identifying customer preferences, sales patterns, and revenue opportunities becomes challenging.

This project was designed to answer key business questions such as:

* Which pizzas generate the highest revenue?
* Which products drive the highest order volume?
* What pizza sizes are most preferred by customers?
* Which days and months generate peak sales?
* Which products require optimization or promotional support?
* How can sales forecasting and inventory planning be improved?

The goal is to provide a centralized analytics solution that supports strategic decision-making and business growth.

## 🔍 Key Business Insights

### Customer Purchasing Behavior

* Highest order volume occurs during weekends.
* Friday and Saturday evenings are peak sales periods.
* Average customers purchase more than two pizzas per order.

### Product Performance

* Classic pizzas are the most popular category.
* Large-sized pizzas contribute the highest share of revenue.
* Certain specialty pizzas show significantly lower demand.

### Revenue Analysis

* Total revenue exceeded $817K.
* Average customer spending is approximately $38 per order.
* Revenue is concentrated among a small group of high-performing products.

### Top Performers

* Thai Chicken Pizza generated the highest revenue.
* Classic Deluxe Pizza recorded the highest sales volume and order count.

### Optimization Opportunities

* Brie Carre Pizza consistently ranked lowest across revenue, quantity sold, and order volume.
* Product optimization and targeted marketing could improve performance.
